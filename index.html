The 7 Environmental Factors Destroying Pipes in Land O Lakes

Florida homes don't fail like homes in other states. Our technicians train specifically for these local conditions:

  1. 1

    Hard Water (7–21 grains/gallon)

    Mineral corrosion causes pinhole leaks in copper and sediment buildup in water heaters.

  2. 2

    Sandy/Shifting Soil

    Underground pipe stress leads to slab leaks and sewer misalignment.

  3. 3

    High Humidity (>70% year-round)

    Mold grows within 24–48 hours after any leak.

  4. 4

    Aging Infrastructure (1970s–1990s)

    Polybutylene and galvanized pipes are ticking time bombs.

  5. 5

    Hurricane Season Flooding

    Overwhelmed sewers back up into homes.

  6. 6

    Aggressive Tree Roots

    Oak and palm roots invade sewer lines seeking nutrients.

  7. 7

    High Water Table

    Shallow groundwater makes slab tunneling difficult and saturates septic fields.
